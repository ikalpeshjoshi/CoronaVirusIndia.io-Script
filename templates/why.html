Why Us?

Here are some frequently asked question (F.A.Q) so that you can trust us.

1. From Where you get Data?

All the data source are from official Indian government website.The website is mohfw.gov.in

2. Is there is any official API?

No there is no any official API from government side. We are using a third party API for data. This third party API scrape data from government official website.

3. How is your website is different from other?

Well it the way of presenting data to the normal people so that they can easily crunch the numbers. It is all about simplicity. We are presenting data in useful way.

4. Is your data is up to date?

Yes, our data is up to date. We update our data on daily basis. This data is live and you can get live update

5. Some website data is different from yours?

There are many websites and it may be possible that they may have different data from us. But our data is official and you can trust us.

6. What are official and unofficial data?

The official data is from Indian government official website and the unofficial data is collected by CovIndia Team.
